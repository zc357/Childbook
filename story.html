Scene 1: Mia's Happy Morning

Mia wakes up in her room, ready to start a new day. She puts on her favorite blue dress, grabs her backpack, and gets ready for school.

Scene 2: Mia Meets a New Friend

On the school playground, Mia meets a new student who looks a little shy. She decides to greet him and invites him to play together.

Scene 3: Playing Together

Mia and her new friend start jumping rope, having a great time. Even though he struggles sometimes, Mia encourages him to keep trying.

Scene 4: Helping a Friend in Need

A friend accidentally loses his backpack, and Mia immediately helps him find it and hands it back to him.

Scene 5: Teamwork

Mia and her friends work together to complete a puzzle, helping each other and finally finishing it successfully.

Scene 6: Learning to Share

During lunch, Mia sees a friend who forgot to bring lunch. She kindly offers to share her sandwich with him.

Scene 7: Solving a Small Disagreement

Mia and her friends have a little argument over a toy. She listens to the teacher's advice, calms down, and they take turns playing, making up in the end.

Scene 8: Celebrating a Birthday

Today is Mia’s birthday. Her friends give her handmade cards and little gifts, and they all share cake, celebrating happily.

Scene 9: Helping Others Together

Mia and her friends work together to collect trash at school, dividing up the tasks to keep the environment clean.

Scene 10: Heading Home

At the end of the day, Mia and her new friends walk home together, promising to see each other again tomorrow. Mia feels very happy because she has gained more friends and made wonderful memories.
